Safety Tips and Guidelines

Resources to help travelers stay safe

General Safety Tips

  • Always verify the identity of your host/guest
  • Keep communication within our platform
  • Read reviews and ratings carefully
  • Trust your instincts

Before Your Stay

  • Review property safety features
  • Save emergency contact numbers
  • Share your itinerary with trusted friends/family
  • Confirm check-in procedures

During Your Stay

  • Familiarize yourself with emergency exits
  • Keep valuable items secure
  • Report any concerns immediately
  • Follow house rules and guidelines
